Lasagna

Description

Zone's Lasagna is not like the other lasagnas. That may be because of the limited choice of meat in disposal in the harsh conditions of Zone. But don't fret, no one will notice that you used rat meat when you mince it.

Ingredients

  • meat 1kg (any meat will do, except zombie's)
  • pasta
  • swamp grass
  • cheese
  • Milk (ask Sidorovich)

Steps

  1. Season the meat with anything you have on hand, then put it on the heated pan and sear until golden
  2. Add cheese and milk
