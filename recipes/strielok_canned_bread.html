Strielok's Canned Bread

Description

Bread, but in a can.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Not "Bread"
  • Tourist's delight
  • Milk (ask Sidorovich)

Steps

  1. Eat the Tourist's delight
  2. Stuff the bread into the leftover can. You don't have to clean it, that way it will acquire some extra taste.
  3. You can eat it with milk, enjoy.
